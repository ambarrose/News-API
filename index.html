About Me

Hi, I'm Ambar Williams, I am currently 27 years old and residing in Upper Hutt, Wellington.

I am a current student at Yoobee school of Design, working toward my Level 6 Diploma in Web and UX Development and looking for an opportunity to begin my career as a Front-end Web Developer and Graphic Designer. My ideal position would be a Front-end App Developer, working in a team to create beautiful and effective UI for users. I enjoy the research and prototype design stages of app development as I can show my creative side while meeting the constraints that have been set by the client.

EXPERIENCE

During my recent studies I have gained knowledge in what it is like to work with a group on various UX design projects and following agile methods to ensure the projects run effectively for all members. In 2015 I had a short term internship at Lime-Red Design in Lower Hutt, Wellington where I was able to demonstrate my graphic design skills in a self directed project that involved meeting and communicating with the client, gathering data and using Adobe products to create consistent page layouts, typographic conventions and styles that met the needs of the client.
